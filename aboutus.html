Nursing Varsity College

We’re committed to providing outstanding nursing services. Our mission is to preserve and develop the healthcare industry.

Contact us

Nursing Varsity College is an accredited private college of which focuses on healthcare training and agricultural training. Nursing Varsity College is not a physical hospital or physical clinic. Nursing Varsity College offers courses which fall under the following faculties :-

Faculty of Healthcare, Faculty of Agriculture, Faculty of Business administration, Faculty of Law, Faculty of Information Technology.

Nursing Varsity College is not Government hospital or Government clinic and does not interfere with the service rendered by the South African Government.

Our Services

At Nursing Varsity College we recruit the students, train the students, send the students out for the in service training to Hospitals ,Clinics , Private healthcare facilities and Public healthcare facilities as well as to both , the public business sector and private business sector.

Our final goal is to try and get all our students employed while they are still fresh and energetic but, this depends on the availability of employment opportunities and the ability of a student to showcase his / her ability to do the targeted job.

As a leading provider of Education and Training in mainly the Healthcare training and Agricultural training band, Nursing Varsity College has much to offer students and prospective partners as an alternative to General Education and Training.

All of our courses are accredited ,recognised, and are in high demand by all sectors of the economy in South Africa and abroad.

Our Vision

To train and produce quality Healthcare graduates.

Nursing assistants and Agricultural graduates who will render professional healthcare services in order to provide cost-effective,quality and competent patient care.

Our Mission

To retain and develop staff for the Healthcare industry as well as the Agricultural sector.

To ensure staff have career development opportunities.

To provide management at all levels with the necessary skills to conduct their work effectively.

To develop a competent workforce.

To work according to necessary legislation governing education, training and development in the healthcare industry.

Our Values

We espouse and subscribe to the principles of:

  • Learner centeredness
  • Lifelong learning

The removal of barriers to accessing learning.

The recognition of prior learning and experience.

The provision of learner support

The maintenance of rigorous quality assurance over the design of learning materials and support systems.

The integration of education and training; and The integration of education and training; and Non-discrimination on the basis of sex, colour, creed or race.
